مَن يُرِدِ اللَّهُ بِهِ خَيْرًا
يُفَقِّهْهُ فِي الدِّينِ

"Whomever Allah intends good for, He grants him understanding in the religion."

This verse from the Quran, "مَن يُرِدِ اللَّهُ بِهِ خَيْرًا يُفَقِّهْهُ فِي الدِّينِ", conveys a profound message about divine guidance and spiritual enlightenment. It emphasizes that those whom Allah wishes to bless with goodness are granted a deep understanding of religion. This understanding goes beyond mere knowledge; it encompasses wisdom, insight, and a profound connection with faith. The verse underscores the importance of seeking comprehension and insight into religious matters as a means to attain goodness in both worldly and spiritual realms. It serves as a reminder of the significance of divine guidance in illuminating the path of believers, guiding them towards righteousness and enlightenment in their journey of faith.
